Introduction

Got7 is a South Korean boy band from JYP Entertainment. Formed by the seven members: JB, Mark, Jinyoung, Jackson, Youngjae, Bambam, and Yugyeom.

They debuted in January 2014 with their first mini album, Got it?, which topped the billborads and charts.
